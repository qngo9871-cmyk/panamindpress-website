About the imprint

The Press

Pana Mind Press is a small independent publisher devoted to a single, broad subject: the life of the mind paid close attention to.

Our list gathers books that take the inner life seriously — whether through the disciplined first-hand record of contemplative practice, the careful scholarship of the Buddhist textual traditions, or fiction that treats consciousness and transformation as worthy of a story’s full weight. We publish across these lines because we believe they illuminate one another: the practitioner’s account, the scholar’s map, and the novelist’s imagination are three approaches to the same territory.

What we look for

Clarity over jargon. Honesty over hype. Work that respects the reader’s intelligence and the seriousness of its subject — books a thoughtful person could return to more than once.

We keep the press deliberately small. Each title is chosen, edited, and produced with care rather than churned to a schedule, and we’d rather publish a few books well than many books quickly.

Where to find the books

Every title in the catalogue is available through Amazon in digital and print editions, with selected titles in hardcover and in translation. For rights, review copies, or other enquiries, please get in touch.
